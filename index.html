Greetings, Cultist!

This page is dedicated to the fans of Rogue Cthulhu. Our games are renowned for player immersion, atmosphere, and fun!

We started 20 years ago as a group of gamers determined to bring the best Call of Cthulhu games to Origins Game Fair and other fantastic gaming conventions. We found ourselves restricted in what and how we could run at the con. We decided to “Go Rogue” and do our own thing. The Fans loved our games, and we continue to this day! We promise you will find at any Rogue Cthulhu game the best GMs, the best stories, and the most enjoyment possible. We have expanded our game repertoire from not only Call of Cthulhu games but all brands of horror. Whatever you crave as far as “Fear of the unknown” goes, we got you covered
